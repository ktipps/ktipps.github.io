So a bit about me...

I use human-centered research to create meaningful user experiences.



Hi! I’m Kara. I’m a user researcher and professional storyteller. I strive to use research to create approachable and inclusive experiences that improve peoples’ lives for the better.


I am currently pursuing my Master of Human of Computer-Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University, focusing on mixed methods user research.


Before I returned to school, I worked as a government consultant and a journalist at places like the BBC and Bloomberg News. I received my bachelors from Georgetown University, where I majored in English and minored in journalism.
